# Who am I and why should you care?

I'll start by answering the second question; you shouldn't! Nothing to see here, move along and get the code you came here for, and enjoy your day ;)

Oh, you're still here? Okay, who actually am I?

Hi. I'm Tristan. A youth leader working at the intersection of community and technology, using code to improve people's lives through games and convenience. I am the founder of my own indie studio Thanium Studios where we make some cool games and help gaming communities develop sites and tools, and the co-founder of the Oceanic Community Games, the largest not-for-profit community-run eSports tournament for mobile games in Australia and Oceania.

Most of my time spent on GitHub is through my work as an Immersive Software Developer alongside the ASTRAL Institute, a youth-led organisation based at Swinburne University that is developing a cohort of inspirational STEM student leaders. You can read all about them here: https://astral.institute/

Anyways, I do a bunch of other cool stuff but I won't bore you to death here, if you're interested in reading more about my escapades feel free to shoot me a follow on LinkedIn! https://www.linkedin.com/in/tristan-than/

Catch ya around!
